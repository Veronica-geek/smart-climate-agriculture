About Us

TerraAly is a web-based Smart Agriculture Information System designed to empower small-scale farmers in adapting to climate change. Developed by Veronica Kariuko Kinyua as part of a Diploma in Business Information Technology at Strathmore University, TerraAly provides real-time weather insights, soil health analysis, and best farming practice recommendations. Our mission is to bridge the gap in access to localized agricultural data, enhancing productivity and resilience while supporting Sustainable Development Goals 2 (Zero Hunger) and 13 (Climate Action).

FAQ

WHAT IS TERRAALY?

TerraAly is a platform that provides farmers with weather data, soil analysis, and farming recommendations to adapt to climate change.

WHO CAN USE TERRAALY?

Small-scale farmers, agricultural experts, and administrators can use TerraAly to enhance farming practices.

HOW DO I ANALYZE MY SOIL?

Input your soil data into our Soil Analysis Module, and the system will provide tailored recommendations.

IS THERE A COST TO USE TERRAALY?

The initial version is free for farmers, with premium features planned for future iterations.
