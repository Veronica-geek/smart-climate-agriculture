Eligibility Criteria

  • Must be a Kenyan citizen with a valid ID or passport.
  • Own or operate a farm of at least 0.5 acres.
  • No adverse credit history with recognized bureaus.
  • Age between 18 and 65 years.
  • Provide proof of farming activity or cooperative membership.

Benefits of Our Credit Service

  • Access to low-interest loans in KSH (starting at 5% p.a.).
  • Flexible repayment plans tailored to your harvest cycles.
  • Support for purchasing equipment, seeds, and irrigation systems.

Important Information

  • Risk Disclosure: Borrowing involves risks, including the possibility of default and loss of collateral. Ensure you can afford repayments before applying.
  • Interest Rates and Fees: Rates start at 5% per annum, with no hidden fees. Late payments may incur penalties up to 2% monthly.
  • Data Privacy: Your information is protected under Kenyan data protection laws. We may share it with credit bureaus for assessment.
  • Loan Terms: Loans range from KSH 1,000 to KSH 500,000, with repayment periods of 6-24 months. Collateral may be required for larger amounts.
  • Default Consequences: Non-payment may result in legal action, credit score impact, and forfeiture of assets.
  • Financial Counseling: We offer free counseling sessions to help with budgeting and repayment planning.
